Shipping policy

Please note that goods are only shipped Monday-Friday, excluding bank holidays. Shipping times begin when you receive the shipping confirmation email.
Please note that once your order is confirmed, we are unable to amend the delivery address or method, or any details within your order, such as product, number of products, size, colour.
If you are not home to receive the delivery, our courier will deliver your parcel to your nearby neighbour or return the item back to our warehouse. If the parcel becomes RTS, please kindly reach out to our customer support to advise whether you would like the order to be re-dispatched or refunded.

We cannot deliver to certain addresses such as student/campus accommodation, hotels, restaurants, stores, or forwarding addresses.

Most of our international orders are shipped on a DDP (Delivery Duties Paid) basis. This means that taxes and duties will be added at the checkout accordingly, and the cost of this depends on the country you are based.

The following countries are shipped to with Delivery Duties Paid:

Australia, Bahrain, Bosnia & Herzegovina, Canada, China, Hong Kong SAR China, Iceland, India, Indonesia, Isle of Man, Israel, Japan, Kuwait, Malaysia, Mexico, New Zealand, Norway, Philippines, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Serbia, Singapore, South Africa, South Korea, Switzerland, Taiwan, Thailand, Turkey, United Arab Emirates, United States, Vietnam.

Any other country or region outside the above list is subject to pay taxes & duties set by their country upon delivery.
Please note, these fees are the sole responsibility of the customer and are non-refundable, even if you return your order.

Click and Collect

If you are local to one of our stores, you are welcome to place an order for collection. To do this, please select the ‘Pick Up’ delivery method when checking out.

To collect your order from a store, you will be required to show:

Confirmation email, official photo ID (passport or driving licence) and payment card used for the order.
